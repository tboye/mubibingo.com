Why MUBI Bingo?

MUBI's streaming catalog varies by country. A film available in the US might not be in Germany, and vice versa. Instead of using a separate search website, MUBI Bingo lets you check availability across 50 countries right on mubi.com.

How it works

  1. Install the extension and pin it to your toolbar
  2. Browse to any film or series on mubi.com
  3. Click the extension to see which countries have it

FAQ

How do I check MUBI availability by country?

Install MUBI Bingo, go to any film or series page on mubi.com, and click the extension icon. You'll see a grid showing which of 50 countries have it available.

Why is a film on MUBI in one country but not another?

MUBI's catalog varies by region due to licensing agreements. A film streaming in the US might not be available in Germany, and vice versa. MUBI Bingo helps you find where any title is available.

What does the availability grid show?

A grid of 50 countries, color-coded: green (streaming now), yellow (coming soon), gray (not available).

Which countries does MUBI Bingo check?

Do I need a MUBI subscription to use this?

No. You don't need to be logged in to MUBI for the extension to work.

Is MUBI Bingo an official MUBI tool?

No. MUBI Bingo is an independent project, not affiliated with or endorsed by MUBI.

Does MUBI Bingo collect my data?

No. Your data stays on your device. Learn more

Is there a keyboard shortcut?

Yes. Ctrl+Shift+0 (Cmd+Shift+0 on Mac).
